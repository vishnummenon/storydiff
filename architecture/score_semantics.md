# Score Semantics

## Consensus Distance

Measures semantic distance from topic consensus.

- **Lower**: closer to cluster consensus
- **Higher**: more divergent

## Framing Polarity

Measures tone or framing intensity relative to cluster norm.

- Should be **carefully defined** in implementation
- May use a **symmetric** or **normalized** scale

## Source Diversity Score

Estimates breadth of perspective or actor representation.

- **Higher**: broader sourcing/perspective coverage

## Novel Claim Score

Measures how much the article emphasizes claims not yet common in the cluster.

- **Higher**: more cluster-distinctive content

## Reliability Score

Represents confidence in system analysis quality.
Factors may include:

- Number of related articles
- Number of unique sources
- Text availability quality
- Assignment confidence
- Consensus stability
